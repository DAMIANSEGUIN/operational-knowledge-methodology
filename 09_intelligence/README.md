# Intelligence System

## Purpose

Build trust and improve the methodology through structured evidence.

## Evidence levels

- A: observed directly in the current organization
- B: repeated across internal assessments or engagements
- C: supported by credible external research
- D: working hypothesis requiring validation

## Required repositories

- Pattern library
- Weak-signal register
- Industry briefs
- Intervention outcomes
- Messaging results
- Qualification decisions
- Human corrections
- Evidence ledger

## Prohibition

Do not publish percentages, benchmarks, or comparisons without sufficient data.
