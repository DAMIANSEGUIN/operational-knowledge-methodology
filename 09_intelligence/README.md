# Intelligence System

## Purpose

Build trust and improve the methodology through structured evidence.

## Evidence levels

- A: observed directly in the current organization
- B: repeated across internal assessments or engagements
- C: supported by credible external research
- D: working hypothesis requiring validation

## Required repositories

- Pattern library
- Weak-signal register
- Industry briefs
- Intervention outcomes
- Messaging results
- Qualification decisions
- Human corrections
- Evidence ledger

## Prohibition

Do not publish percentages, benchmarks, or comparisons without sufficient data.

## Learning Record Standard

Every reusable learning entry must separate:

- observation;
- inference;
- hypothesis;
- recommendation;
- human correction;
- action;
- outcome;
- reusable pattern.

## Pattern Promotion Rule

A pattern may be promoted only when:

- it appears in more than one context, or one context produces strong operational proof;
- its boundaries are documented;
- at least one human correction has been considered;
- the affected module owner is identified;
- `DECISIONS.md` records the promotion when it changes the method.

## Confidence Language

- Low: one signal, useful for questions.
- Medium: repeated signals in one organization.
- High: observed improvement after intervention.
- Reusable: repeated across contexts with clear boundaries.
