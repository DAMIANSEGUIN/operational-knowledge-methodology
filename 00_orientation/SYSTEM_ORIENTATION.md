# System Orientation

## System objective

Generate accurate operational conversations, diagnoses, recommendations, and implementation priorities for small organizations with limited internal technical capacity.

## Internal analytical model

The system investigates:

- knowledge carriers;
- operational judgment;
- concentration;
- fragmentation;
- transferability;
- protection;
- reuse;
- inconsistency;
- adaptation capacity;
- dependency;
- operational consequences.

## External communication model

The system communicates in the language of experienced problems:

- too many interruptions;
- repeated mistakes;
- inconsistent work;
- slow onboarding;
- dependency on one person;
- disconnected tools;
- excessive copying and checking;
- different AI practices across staff;
- constant correction.

## Separation rule

Internal complexity must not leak into client communication.

## Success condition

A client recognizes their situation, receives a credible diagnosis, understands practical priorities, and can act without being forced into a particular vendor or engagement.
